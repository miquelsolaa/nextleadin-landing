Hunter.io is strong for email lookup. Lusha provides broader contact data. NextLeadIn focuses on segmentation and qualification to sell better.

## When to choose Hunter.io

- You need verified emails quickly.
- You want a simple, low-cost tool.

## When to choose Lusha

- You need phone numbers and extra contact data.
- You run outbound prospecting at scale.

## When to choose NextLeadIn

- You prioritize segmentation and qualified leads.
- You need context to personalize outreach.

## Recommendation

Hunter.io and Lusha are useful for point data. NextLeadIn adds a layer of quality and sales readiness.
