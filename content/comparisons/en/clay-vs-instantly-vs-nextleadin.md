## Overview

Clay is ideal for advanced enrichment. Instantly is for email volume. NextLeadIn optimizes lead selection and conversation readiness.

## When to choose Clay

- You need a flexible data engine.
- You have time to build workflows.

## When to choose Instantly

- You need fast email volume.
- Email is your main channel.

## When to choose NextLeadIn

- You want segmentation and prioritization before outreach.
- You need context to improve responses.

## Recommendation

Instantly is for volume, Clay for data. NextLeadIn balances quality and sales action.
