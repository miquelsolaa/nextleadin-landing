## Overview

Cognism is strong on quality and compliance. ZoomInfo is an enterprise platform with wide coverage. NextLeadIn focuses on segmentation and AI reports.

| Criteria | Cognism | ZoomInfo | NextLeadIn |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| GDPR compliance | High | High | High |
| Intent data | Medium | High | AI context |
| Complexity | Medium | High | Low |
| Lead quality | High | High | High and segmented |
| AI reports | No | No | Yes |
| Focus | Data | Enterprise | Conversations |

## When to choose Cognism

- You need GDPR-compliant data in Europe.
- You prioritize verified data quality.

## When to choose ZoomInfo

- You operate at enterprise scale with wide coverage.
- You have budget for a complex rollout.

## When to choose NextLeadIn

- You want precise segmentation and lead context.
- You need a faster path to meetings.

## Recommendation

For B2B teams seeking quality and speed, NextLeadIn is more efficient than large enterprise platforms.
