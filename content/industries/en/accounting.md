## Why NextLeadIn is perfect for selling to accounting firms

The accounting and advisory sector is one of the most stable and profitable for B2B sales. These are clients with high LTV who, once they trust a supplier, maintain the relationship for years.

### Data you'll find for each firm

- **Name and address** of the firm
- **Verified phone** (partner and office)
- **Contact email**
- **Specialty**: tax, payroll, accounting, legal...
- **Opening hours**
- **Rating** and number of reviews
- **Estimated size**: small, medium, large
- **Potential scoring**

### Common use cases

- **Accounting management software**: find firms with outdated systems
- **Electronic invoicing platforms**: detect practices that need to upgrade
- **Professional training**: segment by specialty and size
- **Digitalization services**: identify firms with manual processes
- **Payroll software**: find practices with many payroll clients
- **Professional marketing**: detect firms with low visibility
