## Why NextLeadIn is perfect for selling to real estate agencies

The real estate sector is one of the most profitable for B2B sales. Agencies constantly invest in marketing, technology, and services. But reaching them is difficult because agents are always out of the office.

### Data you'll find for each agency

- **Name and address** of the agency
- **Verified phone** (office and agent mobiles)
- **Contact email**
- **Type**: traditional agency, franchise, developer, independent agent...
- **Portfolio**: number of active listings
- **Specialty**: residential, commercial, luxury, rental...
- **Rating** and number of reviews
- **Potential scoring**

### Common use cases

- **Real estate CRM software**: find agencies with manual processes
- **Property portals**: detect agencies with low online visibility
- **Photography and video**: identify agencies with low-quality listings
- **Home staging**: find agencies with hard-to-sell properties
- **Energy certificates**: segment by portfolio size
- **Legal services**: identify agencies with high transaction activity
