## Why NextLeadIn is perfect for selling to gyms

The fitness sector is highly diverse and fragmented. From large chains with hundreds of locations to small neighborhood boutique studios. NextLeadIn lets you segment and find exactly the type of gym that fits your offering.

### Data you'll find for each gym

- **Name and address** of the center
- **Verified phone** number
- **Contact email**
- **Center type**: gym, crossfit, yoga, pilates, etc.
- **Opening hours**
- **Rating** and number of reviews
- **Review analysis**: issues detected by AI
- **Potential scoring**

### Common use cases

- **Fitness equipment**: find gyms with equipment complaints
- **Management software**: detect centers with booking or payment issues
- **Cleaning services**: identify gyms with cleanliness complaints
- **Digital marketing**: find centers with low online presence
- **Nutrition and supplements**: segment by gym type (crossfit, bodybuilding, etc.)
- **Maintenance and renovations**: detect centers with outdated facilities
