## Why NextLeadIn is perfect for selling to veterinarians

The pet industry is one of the fastest-growing in Spain. Pet owners are spending more on their animals, and veterinary clinics are investing in equipment and services.

### Data you'll find for each veterinary clinic

- **Name and address** of the clinic
- **Verified phone** (clinic and vet's mobile)
- **Contact email**
- **Services**: general consultation, surgery, diagnostics, emergency...
- **Specialty**: small animals, exotic, equine...
- **Opening hours** (including 24h emergency)
- **Rating** and number of reviews
- **Potential scoring**

### Common use cases

- **Veterinary equipment**: find clinics that need to upgrade
- **Clinic management software**: detect vets with manual processes
- **Food and supplements**: segment by animal types they treat
- **Diagnostic laboratories**: identify clinics that outsource testing
- **Pet insurance**: find clinics with high client volume
- **Veterinary training**: segment by specialty and services
