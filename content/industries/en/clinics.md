## Why NextLeadIn is perfect for selling to clinics

The healthcare sector is one of the hardest to prospect. Clinics have reception staff trained to filter calls and decision-makers are hard to reach. NextLeadIn gives you direct access to contact data and context to prepare a relevant pitch.

### Data you'll find for each clinic

- **Name and address** of the center
- **Verified phone** number
- **Contact email**
- **Specialty**: dental, ophthalmology, dermatology, physical therapy, etc.
- **Office hours**
- **Rating** and number of reviews
- **Review analysis**: issues detected by AI
- **Potential scoring**

### Common use cases

- **Practice management software**: find clinics with appointment or billing issues
- **Medical equipment**: detect centers with outdated equipment
- **Specialized cleaning services**: identify clinics with hygiene standards
- **Healthcare marketing**: find clinics with low online visibility
- **Training and certifications**: segment by specialty
- **Maintenance services**: detect centers with facility issues
