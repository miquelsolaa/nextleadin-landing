## Why NextLeadIn is perfect for selling to academies

The training sector has grown enormously post-COVID. Academies need technology, marketing, and services to compete. It's a market in transformation with many opportunities.

### Data you'll find for each academy

- **Name and address** of the center
- **Verified phone** (management and administration)
- **Contact email**
- **Training type**: languages, exam prep, tutoring, music...
- **Opening hours**
- **Rating** and number of reviews
- **Digital presence**: website, online platform
- **Potential scoring**

### Common use cases

- **E-learning platforms**: find academies without online training
- **Academic management software**: detect centers with manual processes
- **Teaching materials**: segment by training type
- **Education marketing**: identify academies with low visibility
- **School furniture**: find centers that need renovation
- **Certification services**: segment by exam types
