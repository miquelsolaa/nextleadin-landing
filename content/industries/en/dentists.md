## Why NextLeadIn is perfect for selling to dentists

The dental sector is one of the largest in private healthcare. Dental clinics constantly invest in equipment, technology, and marketing. But reaching the practice owner is difficult.

### Data you'll find for each dental clinic

- **Name and address** of the clinic
- **Verified phone** (clinic and owner's mobile)
- **Contact email**
- **Specialties**: orthodontics, implants, cosmetic, endodontics...
- **Services** offered
- **Opening hours**
- **Rating** and number of reviews
- **Review analysis**: issues detected by AI
- **Potential scoring**

### Common use cases

- **Dental equipment**: find clinics that need to upgrade
- **Dental supplies**: segment by specialty and volume
- **Clinic management software**: detect practices with manual processes
- **Dental laboratories**: identify clinics with high work volume
- **Dental marketing**: find clinics with low online visibility
- **Specialized training**: segment by specialties
