## Why NextLeadIn is perfect for selling to hotels

The tourism sector represents 12% of Spain's GDP. Hotels constantly invest in improvements, technology, and services. But reaching decision-makers is difficult because reception filters all sales calls.

### Data you'll find for each hotel

- **Name and address** of the establishment
- **Verified phone** (management, not just reception)
- **Contact email** of the director/manager
- **Category**: star rating, accommodation type
- **Capacity**: number of rooms
- **Chain** or independent
- **Rating** and number of reviews
- **Review analysis**: issues detected by AI
- **Potential scoring**

### Common use cases

- **Amenities and consumables**: find hotels that need suppliers
- **Property management software (PMS)**: detect hotels with manual processes
- **Commercial laundry**: identify high-occupancy hotels
- **Maintenance and renovations**: find hotels with outdated facilities
- **Tourism marketing**: detect hotels with low online visibility
- **Room equipment**: identify hotels undergoing renovation
