## Why NextLeadIn is perfect for selling to hair salons

The hair and beauty industry is one of the largest in terms of local businesses in Spain, with over 55,000 establishments. It's a fragmented market where traditional prospecting is highly inefficient.

### Data you'll find for each salon

- **Name and address** of the salon
- **Verified phone** number
- **Contact email**
- **Service type**: hair salon, beauty center, barber shop, spa...
- **Opening hours**
- **Rating** and number of reviews
- **Review analysis**: issues detected by AI
- **Potential scoring**

### Common use cases

- **Hair products**: find salons that need new suppliers
- **Management software**: detect salons with booking issues
- **Salon equipment**: identify centers with outdated furniture
- **Professional training**: segment by salon type and size
- **Beauty products**: find centers offering specific treatments
- **Digital marketing**: identify salons with low online presence
