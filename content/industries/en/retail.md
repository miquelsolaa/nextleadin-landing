## Why NextLeadIn is perfect for selling to retail stores

The retail sector is the largest in terms of local businesses. With over 180,000 stores in our database, it's a huge but highly fragmented market. NextLeadIn lets you segment and find exactly the type of store you need.

### Data you'll find for each store

- **Name and address** of the business
- **Verified phone** of the owner
- **Contact email**
- **Sector**: fashion, food, electronics, home...
- **Opening hours**
- **Rating** and number of reviews
- **Digital presence**: website, social media
- **Review analysis**: issues detected by AI
- **Potential scoring**

### Common use cases

- **POS and management software**: find stores with outdated systems
- **E-commerce and marketplaces**: detect stores without online sales
- **Product suppliers**: segment by sector and volume
- **Signage and branding**: find stores with outdated image
- **Security systems**: identify stores in commercial areas
- **Digital marketing**: detect stores with low online presence
