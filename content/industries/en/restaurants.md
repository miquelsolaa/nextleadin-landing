## Why NextLeadIn is perfect for selling to restaurants

Restaurants are a unique segment: they have very limited digital presence on traditional B2B platforms like LinkedIn, but are highly visible on Google Maps and review platforms.

NextLeadIn leverages this reality to give you access to a database no other platform has.

### Data you'll find for each restaurant

- **Name and address** of the business
- **Verified phone** (landline and mobile when available)
- **Contact email**
- **Opening hours**
- **Rating** and number of reviews
- **Review analysis**: pain points detected by AI
- **Potential scoring**: estimated size, growth, opportunity

### Common use cases

- **POS and management software**: find restaurants that need to modernize
- **Cleaning and maintenance services**: detect restaurants with cleanliness complaints
- **Food suppliers**: segment by cuisine type
- **Marketing and social media**: identify restaurants with low online presence
- **Delivery services**: find restaurants without their own delivery
