## Why NextLeadIn is perfect for selling to driving schools

The driving school sector is traditional and needs modernization. With increasing competition and regulatory changes, driving schools are looking to differentiate and improve.

### Data you'll find for each driving school

- **Name and address** of the center
- **Verified phone** (office and owner's mobile)
- **Contact email**
- **Licenses**: car, motorcycle, truck, bus, commercial...
- **Opening hours**
- **Rating** and number of reviews
- **Pass rate** (when available)
- **Potential scoring**

### Common use cases

- **Training vehicles**: find schools that need to renew their fleet
- **Management software**: detect schools with manual processes
- **Online test platforms**: identify schools without digital training
- **Insurance**: segment by license types and volume
- **Local marketing**: find schools with low visibility
- **Signage and teaching materials**: segment by size
