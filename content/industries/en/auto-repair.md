## Why NextLeadIn is perfect for selling to auto repair shops

The automotive sector is one of the hardest to prospect. Owners are always busy, many shops have no website, and competition for these clients is high. NextLeadIn gives you direct access to verified contacts.

### Data you'll find for each shop

- **Name and address** of the shop
- **Verified phone** (landline and mobile when available)
- **Contact email**
- **Specialty**: mechanics, body work, electrical, tires...
- **Brands** they service (official or multi-brand)
- **Opening hours**
- **Rating** and number of reviews
- **Review analysis**: issues detected by AI
- **Potential scoring**

### Common use cases

- **Parts and supplies**: find shops that need reliable suppliers
- **Diagnostic equipment**: detect shops with outdated tools
- **Management software**: identify shops with billing issues
- **Lubricants and chemicals**: segment by volume and specialty
- **Technical training**: find shops that need updates
- **Insurance**: identify shops with high body work activity
