## **Why Global Platforms Fall Short for Spanish SMEs**

Global prospecting platforms are built on vast, international datasets, but their coverage of highly localized, small-to-medium Spanish businesses is often sparse or inaccurate. They typically aggregate data from corporate registries, social media, and web scraping, which works well for large, digitally-savvy companies. However, for a family-run restaurant in Valencia or a local law firm in Bilbao, this data is often incomplete, outdated, or simply non-existent within their databases. This leads to frustrated sales teams, high bounce rates for email campaigns, and unproductive cold calls. The cost-per-lead also escalates significantly when manually sifting through irrelevant data or buying unverified lists, often reaching €2-5 per qualified lead.
