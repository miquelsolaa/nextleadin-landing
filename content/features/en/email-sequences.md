## How Email Sequences Work

NextLeadIn lets you create automated email sequences to complement your cold calls.

### Intuitive Sequence Creation

Design multi-step sequences with a visual editor. Define the content of each email and the wait time between steps.

### Automatic Personalization

Each email is sent with the lead's data inserted automatically. Your message looks handwritten but sends at scale.

### Enrollment from Lead Table

Select one or multiple leads and enroll them in a sequence with one click. Automation takes care of the rest.

### Real-Time Metrics

Check anytime how many emails have been sent, opened, clicked, and replied to. Optimize your sequences with real data.
