## How Cold Calling Works

NextLeadIn puts the phone at the center of your sales workflow. Local businesses respond better to calls than cold emails.

### Click-to-Call from Anywhere

Don't waste time copying numbers. From the lead table or detail, click the phone and call directly.

### Complete Call Logging

After each call, log the result with one click. Add duration and notes if needed. Everything stays in the lead history.

### Smart Follow-up System

When a lead asks you to call later, schedule the follow-up and forget about it. The system will alert you when it's time to contact again.

### Scripts by Customer Profile

Create specific scripts for each business type (restaurants, shops, services...). When you open a lead, you'll have the perfect pitch ready.
