## How Lead Management Works

NextLeadIn offers a powerful and intuitive lead table designed for sales teams working with local businesses.

### Smart Lead Table

View all your leads in a table with customizable columns. Sort by any field, filter by multiple criteria, and search instantly.

### AI-Powered Scoring

Each lead receives an automatic score based on criteria you define. AI analyzes sector, location, business volume, and other factors to help you prioritize.

### Personalized Call Reports

Before each call, check the AI-generated report with all the context you need: what the business does, what problems it might have, and how to present your solution.

### Notes & History

Add notes to each lead with the rich text editor. All activity is recorded to maintain a complete history of each relationship.
