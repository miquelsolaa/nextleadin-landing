## How Pipeline and Analytics Work

NextLeadIn gives you a complete view of your sales process with actionable metrics.

### Interactive Kanban Pipeline

View all your leads organized by status in columns. Move them with drag & drop and see at a glance where you have the most opportunities.

### Analytics Dashboard

Check key metrics in real-time: calls made by outcome, emails sent and opened, conversion funnel, and much more.

### Best Hours to Call

Discover which hours and days work best for your leads. Optimize your calling schedule with real data.

### Complete Export

Export all leads with all their data to CSV. Import them to other tools or do advanced analysis with Excel or Google Sheets.
